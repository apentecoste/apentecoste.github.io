Consequences for Host countries

Data Source: [UNHCR 2018][World Happiness Report 2019][IMF World Data 2018] [Freedom of Speech Index 2019]

As people of concern transferred to asylumn countries, it is important to analyze the social and economical impact to hosting countries. We are focusing on analyze the impact on top 6 countries in terms of total refugee population in 2018.

The preconceived idea of refugee always cause negative effects can prove to be wrong. Moreover, it even have more positive effect to developed countries. In general, by looking at the happiness index, the refugee population does not has much influence on people reside in hosting countries in terms of satisfaction level toward their lives. In terms of economic impact, the countries that show largely increase in inflation and unemployment are Turkey and Iran, which both more likely caused by the economical crisis or financial sanctions that can also explain the change in happiness index for the two countries. As for highly developed country like Germany,there's no clear indicator for negative influence but a decreased unemployment rate. The charts show that to asylumn countries, there's less negative effects for countries that are more economically advanced and even postive effect such as bringing more labor force for countries have aging population.
